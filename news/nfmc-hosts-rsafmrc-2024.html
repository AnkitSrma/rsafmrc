Milestone · 2024

NFMC is hosting Regional South Asia Fire Management Resource Center

Under an agreement signed by GFMC and NFMC on 13 April 2024, the Nepal Forest Fire Management Chapter now hosts the Regional South Asia Fire Management Resource Center.

On 13 April 2024, the Global Fire Monitoring Center (GFMC), Freiburg, Germany, and the Nepal Forest Fire Management Chapter (NFMC), Kathmandu, Nepal, signed an agreement establishing NFMC as the host of the Regional South Asia Fire Management Resource Center.

NFMC, registered with the Government of Nepal since 2008 and established in line with the objectives of the UNDRR Wildland Fire Advisory Group (WFAG) and the Global Wildland Fire Network (GWFN), will collaborate with national and regional scientific institutions, land, landscape and forest management agencies, and civil society representatives across Nepal and neighboring countries of South Asia.

Under the agreement, GFMC and NFMC will jointly develop a programme covering capacity building, advanced landscape fire management training, cross-border cooperation, and the dissemination of scientific-technical and satellite-derived data on landscape fires, with particular outreach to Bhutan and India.

Mr. Sundar Prasad Sharma will serve as Head of the Regional South Asia Fire Management Resource Center. The agreement runs through the end of 2030 and may be extended by mutual consent.

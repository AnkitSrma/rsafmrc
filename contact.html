Let’s shape the next phase of RSAFMRC together.

The Regional South Asia Wildland Fire Network is convened by Nepal's Department of Forests, with GFMC serving as technical secretariat.

Convening structure

  • Convening country: Nepal (Department of Forests)
  • Coordinating secretariat: Global Fire Monitoring Center (GFMC)
  • Member countries: Bangladesh, Bhutan, India, Nepal, Sri Lanka
  • Parent network: UNISDR Global Wildland Fire Network

Get in touch

National focal point institutions, partner organizations, and researchers are welcome to reach out to the network's coordinating secretariat to discuss collaboration and regional fire management activities.
